About

Lokalcoins TO BE SIMPLE An API to fetch price of cryptocurrency in different fiat currencies. It's a combination of Binance.com API and exchangeratesapi.io API. Currently it supports only 33 fiat currencies published by the European Central Bank and 363 Cryptocurrencies out of total listed in Binance.
